From rotating Scrum Master duties at Jadu while shipping CMS features, to introducing Agile at Leeds Building Society as a hands-on senior developer, to running Scrum at IMA-HOME while engineering frontends for Adidas and MARS — every leadership step was taken without leaving the codebase behind. My leadership arc has grown continuously since 2017: from rotating Scrum Master roles at Jadu and Leeds Building Society, to bootstrapping and leading the Content Capability team at Utility Warehouse, to my current Engineering Manager role — which at peak oversaw two teams and <span class="stat">8 engineers</span>, and through three restructurings now leads a specialist <span class="stat">3-person</span> team covering <span class="stat">six critical platform services</span> across the uw.co.uk estate. Each step a deliberate expansion of scope, and each one an added layer of responsibility on top of the technical work I never set down.
